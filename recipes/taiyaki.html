Taiyaki

Description

Japanese style waffle or cake-like snack.

Shaped into the form of a fish and filled with red bean paste.

Inspired by this guide.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups of cake flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 large egg
  • 200ml (3/4 cup + 4 tsp) whole milk
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 5 Tbsp of filling (can be red bean paste or something else)
  • 1 Tbsp of neutral oil

Steps on Making the Batter

  1. Sift cake flour, baking powder, and baking soda into a large bowl.
  2. Add sugar and whisk.
  3. In a medium bowl, add egg.
  4. Whisk the egg.
  5. Add milk to egg whisk.
  6. Pour contents of medium bowl into the large bowl.
  7. Whisk more.
  8. Keep batter in fridge for one hour.
  9. Pour batter into a measuring cup (probably optional).

Steps on Cooking the Taiyaki.

  1. Preheat the taiyaki maker over medium-low heat.
  2. Fill the taiyaki mold about 60% of the batter.
  3. In the center of each mold, put about 1 Tbsp of the filling.
  4. Pour more batter on top of the fillings to cover them.
  5. Close the lid and immediately flip.
  6. Cook for 2 to 2 1/2 minutes on one side.
  7. Flip the maker over and cook on the other side for same time.
  8. Open the maker and check if the taiyaki is golden brown.
  9. Remove them from the pan and leave them on a wire rack to cool.
  10. Make more until you run out of batter or filling.
